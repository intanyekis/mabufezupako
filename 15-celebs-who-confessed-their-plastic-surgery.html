After all, isn’t that why celebrities go out and get work done, even before they require it? It’s bred out of fear. They are so scared to lose their looks that they are willing to do anything to stay young. But getting plastic surgery can be terrifying- especially since there are no guarantees that it will be any better. Or worse, that it won’t end up changing their appearance so completely that they are unrecognizable. Poor Jennifer Grey, she took her Dirty Dancing paycheck and wanted to fix the bump on her nose. She came out unrecognizable; she was beautiful, but she had a hard time getting cast in any movies after because no one knew who she was.

For the most part, celebrities don’t like to talk about the surgeries that they have. They like to keep that on the down-low and hope that we don’t notice. It takes a pretty skilled plastic surgeon, however, to be able to pull that off; though it has been done. Check out the celebrity confessions below of celebrities who have done the deed and lived to tell about it.
