Who is Adrian Chiles?

Adrian is a British television and radio presenter, currently working BBC Radio 5 Live. He was a chief presenter for football coverage on ITV Sport from 2010 until 2015.

Adrian graduated from Haybridge High School. Later, he got a degree in literature from the University of London. Adrian was a presenter in Working Lunch from 1994 to 2007, The Apprentice: You're Fired!, Drinkers Like Me, in 2018, Match of the Day 2 from 2004 to 2010 and ITV Sport from 2010 to 2015.

He also co-presented Wake Up to Money on BBC Radio 5 Live, with English Financial journalist, Mickey Clark.
