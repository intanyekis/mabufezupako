Fans are about to break the internet with all of the comments coming through social media, as it seems Kanye West has just admitted to cheating on Kim Kardashian. To make matters even worse, he seems to admit that he cheated on her after she fathered two of his children.

The music mogul initially confused fans, who took the lyrics at face value and assumed the Hurricane lyrics were in reference to a fling Kanye West had with Irina Shayk. On the contrary. Closer examination of the words has now led fans to believe that this song is an admission of his guilt, his infidelity, and of him being the reason that Kimye seized to be.

Understandably, Twitter has erupted.

Kanye West's Revealing Lyrics

Donda was supposed to be released much sooner than it was,and West revealed not one, but three listening parties to tease fans along the way. After many delayed release dates, fans were finally able to sink their teeth into the lyrics and the beats of Kanye West's most highly anticipated album. While they were doing so, critics pulled some lyrics out of Hurricane that seem to not be about Irina Shayk at all.

The lyrics are; “Here I go actin’ too rich / Here I go with a new chick / And I know what the truth is / Still playin’ after two kids / It’s a lot to digest when your life always movin’.”

Kanye has made reference to his inability to remain faithful before, namely in his 2010 song, Runaway, in which he says; "See, I could have me a good girl, And still be addicted to them hood rats, And I just blame everything on you, At least you know that's what I'm good at.."

Now that the lyrics are about cheating on Kim Kardashian while married, fans are really paying attention and digging deeper to learn more.
