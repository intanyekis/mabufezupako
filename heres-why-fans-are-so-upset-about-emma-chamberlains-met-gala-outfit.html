The Controversy Over Emma's Met Gala Outfit And Her Reaction

Fast-forward to the 2022 Met Gala that happened just this month. Chamberlain attended as an interviewer on the red carpet. The theme for this year was 'Gilded Glamour'. She wore a two-piece Louis Vuitton outfit and a full tiara that complimented her bleach blonde bob. Chamberlain was signed to Cartier as an ambassador, so it made sense to see her sporting a beautiful necklace designed by the brand on the carpet.

The huge problem with the outfit was that exact Cartier-designed necklace. The necklace has a historical past as it was notably a possession of Indian royalty, Maharaja Bhupinder Singh of Patiala. This necklace suddenly went missing around 1948 and is believed to have been stolen by the British because it showed up in a London jewelry store in the '90s. Which is where it was bought by Cartier.
