HYBE Will Be Having a Multi-Label Worldwide Audition In The US

According to the K-pop agency, seven of HYBE’s music labels will be looking for talent in the United States this upcoming month.

For the three categories of voice, rap, and dance, anyone between the ages of 11 and 19 may apply. The firm anticipates that international talent, particularly from North America, will actively participate in the auditions.

On April 8, 9, 15, and 16, multi-label auditions will be held in Las Vegas. The seven companies taking part in the tryouts include Big Hit Music, Source Music, HYBE Labels Japan, HYBE America, Pledis Entertainment, Belift Lab, and KOZ Entertainment. One of the divisions of the corporation is Big Hit Music, which is responsible for BTS.

How to Apply for the HYBE Global Audition 2022 and the Eligibility Criteria

Applications can be submitted by clicking a link that leads to a Google form on the official Instagram or YouTube channels. It expires on April 4, 2022.

It is necessary to enter the standard information such as name, email, performance category (singing, rapping, dancing), nationality, gender, birth year, phone number, social media profile links, and a front-facing photo.

Additionally, applicants may include the URL of a video clip of their act. The agency’s multi-label audition is open to world citizens of both sexes between the ages of 11 and 19.

Young musicians who stand out in the auditions will have the chance to develop into well-known performers around the world with the help of world-class professionals from various labels.

For additional information, check out the website and the official Instagram feed of the agency audition.

Details Regarding The K-Pop Agency

A new female group featuring Miyawaki Sakura and Kim Chaewon will debut in May, according to the K-pop agency. On March 21, 2022, the record label announced its launch. The new female group members were personally welcomed by Bang Si-hyuk, the creator of Big Hit Music and several other businesses, via a social media post.

Since the record label is home to several of the greatest music groups in history, including the Grammy-nominated international boy group BTS from Big Hit Music, it is the dream of K-pop fans worldwide to work for this agency.
