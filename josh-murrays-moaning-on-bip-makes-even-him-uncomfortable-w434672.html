Josh Murray's Moaning on 'BIP' Makes Even Him 'Uncomfortable'

July 18, 2024

No editing needed! Josh Murray, like the rest of Bachelor Nation, knows that he has a moaning problem. The Bachelor in Paradise star joked about his strange kissing habit while watching the ABC series on Monday, August 15.

“Passion and attraction is a beautiful thing,” Murray, 32, wrote via Twitter. “But even I am a little uncomfortable with how much they replay my moan for every make out.”

Murray usually leaves the moaning for one woman: Amanda Stanton.

Murray and Stanton, 26, immediately hit it off after he stole her away from Nick Viall. The men, who are longtime enemies, were the final two during Andi Dorfman‘s season 10 of The Bachelorette. Murray received the final rose, but the couple called off their engagement five months after the finale aired.

The former pro baseball player has clearly moved on since the split. He and Stanton often have heavy, loud make out sessions during BIP, much to the dismay of their fellow cast members.

“Take it or leave it, always gonna be myself no matter what,” Murray tweeted on Monday. “Genuinely wish happiness for all those on this show, they all deserve it.”

Murray wasn’t always supportive of his costars. While filming the show, Murray took issue with Evan Bass. During Monday’s episode, the erectile dysfunction specialist interrupted an intimate moment between Murray and Stanton to ask her on a date. She declined, and Bass later warned her about claims that Dorfman made against Murray, which included verbal abuse allegations. Murray, after moaning while eating a cheese pizza, took Bass aside to hash things out.
