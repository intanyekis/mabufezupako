Kendall Jenner was born into fame, and whether she liked it at the time or not, her mother, Kris Jenner, had her thrust into the spotlight at a very young age. Forbes reports that she was just 11 years old at the time that the family let camera crews into their home and began filming Keeping Up With The Kardashians, and she's remained in the spotlight since then. Kendall Jenner tends not to flaunt her lifestyle as much as her sisters do, and is the more laid back one in the bunch, but don't be fooled by her more low-key personality. The 25 year old supermodel is a brilliant business woman, who with the help of her 'momager' Kris Jenner, has amassed an impressive $45 million net worth.

Being part of one of the most famous families in the world definitely comes with some perks. As a trade-off for allowing cameras to follow her every move and document some of her most personal moments, Kendall Jenner is said to be making 7 figures for her time she invests in the reality television show, Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

This is especially impressive, considering that between all the other siblings, and all the things they have going on in their lives, Kendall isn't the most featured on the show. She spends less time filming this program than the rest of the Kardashian-Jenner girls, and still brings home the same 7-figure income that they do.

Endorsements

Kendall Jenner is no stranger to the lucrative income that can be obtained through simple endorsements. She has been connected to a series of endorsement deals, each of which pays her handsomely to become the face of their brand.

Her most famous endorsement deal was her controversial ad for Pepsi, which was seemingly a great idea until it quickly turned into a controversial uproar. When the commercial aired, many viewers felt that it mocked and minimized the struggles of Black citizens and the real issues surrounding police brutality. In spite of the epic fail and the tone-deaf nature of the ad itself, Kendall made a fortune on the endorsement deal, with People citing that she earned somewhere in the range of $400,000 - $1 million for the failed ad campaign.

She has also been linked to a variety of advertisement and endorsement deals for major brands such as Adidas, Fendi, Estée Lauder, Marc Jacobs, and Calvin Klein. Kendall gets paid so well for these endorsements that when combined, her endorsement deals pay out more than anything else that she does, earning her millions of dollars to add towards her already massive net worth.

#Sponcon

Not everything that Jenner touches is a huge success when it's out there in the world, and her endorsement deal with Pepsi was an example of this. She happened to earn some other forms of highly controversial income as well. Fans had more questions than answers when they found out that Kendall Jenner had been paid a whopping $250,000 to endorse the Fyre Festival in 2017. For those who are unaware, that was her one-time payment for one simple promotion she posted on her Instagram page, and sadly, the entire event ended up being a scam. The festival promised a series of performances by huge artists as well as luxury stays at hotels and food service, etc. As it turns out, she was paid to promote a scam - the artists did not show up at all, and fans were left with far-less than the luxurious accommodations they had been promised.

Modeling

Kendall Jenner has gone from being a reality TV star based on the cultivated success of her family name, to being a full-blown, internationally acclaimed supermodel in her own right. She has managed to establish a professional modeling career for herself that is completely independent of her family's success, and has soared to new heights with this career move. Forbes has confirmed that Kendall is the highest paid supermodel in the entire world, beating out the likes of Bella Hadid, Gigi Hadid, and even Gisele Bundchen.

To put things in perspective, Kendall demands a jaw-dropping $22 million for her modeling appearances, as compared to the competition which Cheatsheet reports as being; "Gisel Bündchen, who made $17.5 million for her modeling career in 2017 Chrissy Teigen, bringing in $13.5 million and Adriana Lima at $10.5 million. Gigi Hadid came in at $9.5 million for the year."
