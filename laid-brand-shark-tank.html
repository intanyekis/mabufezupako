Who founded Laid Brand?

Laid Brand was founded by Adam Rauch, David Hensley, and Derek Shaw. The three founders recognized a significant gap in the hair care product market and saw an opportunity to develop a line of products that would not only improve the appearance of hair but also boost self-confidence.

“We wanted to create high-quality hair care products that would not only enhance the beauty of hair but also empower individuals to feel confident and attractive,” says Adam Rauch, one of the Laid Brand founders.

Adam and David, with their expertise in the beauty industry, believed that they could fill this market gap and provide customers with innovative and effective hair care solutions. They were determined to create products that would not only address common hair concerns but also harness the power of pheromones to enhance attractiveness and appeal.

David Hensley: A Visionary in Hair Care

David Hensley, another co-founder of Laid Brand, brought his extensive knowledge and experience in hair care to the table. With a background as a renowned hairstylist and salon owner, David understood the challenges individuals face when it comes to maintaining healthy and beautiful hair.

Derek Shaw, the third co-founder, joined Laid Brand later on and contributed his expertise in business strategy and operations, ensuring the company’s growth and success in the highly competitive hair care market.

Laid Brand Shark Tank Pitch

During their appearance on Shark Tank, Adam and Derek presented their business, Laid Brand, and pitched for a $30,000 investment in exchange for a 30% stake in the company. This valuation placed Laid Brand at $100,000. The entrepreneurs highlighted their initial sales, which amounted to $27,000 from salon and online channels.

However, despite their early success, the founders revealed that they had been reformulating their product, resulting in a temporary halt in sales. This decision was driven by their commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. It was during this period that they approached the Sharks for an investment to support their efforts.

During the pitch, the Sharks inquired about market research and the existence of a pheromone community interested in the products. Adam and Derek shared that they had carried out comprehensive market research, identifying a demand for hair care products infused with pheromones. They also noted the existence of a pheromone community who expressed keen interest in their offerings.

Ultimately, despite their compelling pitch and promising initial sales, the founders of Laid Brand did not secure an investment from the Sharks. Nonetheless, their appearance on Shark Tank provided them with valuable exposure and the opportunity to showcase their innovative product to a wide audience.
