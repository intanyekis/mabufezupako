Who Is Madonna?

Madonna is a singer, songwriter, and actress from the United States. Madonna is known as the "Queen of Pop" for her constant reinvention and variety in music production, composition, and visual presentation.

She has pushed the frontiers of artistic expression in mainstream music while remaining in complete control of her career. Her writings, which include social, political, sexual, and religious themes, have sparked debate as well as critical recognition.
