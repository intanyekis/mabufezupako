Introduction to Loverboy

Loverboy is a Canadian rock band formed in 1979 in Calgary, Alberta. The band gained immense popularity in the 1980s with their high-energy performances and catchy tunes. Comprised of lead vocalist Mike Reno, guitarist Paul Dean, keyboardist Doug Johnson, bassist Scott Smith, and drummer Matt Frenette, Loverboy quickly captivated audiences with their infectious blend of rock and pop. Their music resonated with listeners around the world, making them one of the most successful bands of the era.
