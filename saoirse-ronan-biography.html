Who is Saoirse Ronan?

Saoirse Ronan is an Irish and American actress. Saoirse Ronan came into the limelight after appearing in the 2007 movie Atonement. Furthermore, her notable works include the City of Ember, The Lovely Bones, The Way Back, and a few more.

Saoirse has also played the role of Agatha in the 2014 mega-hit The Grand Budapest Hotel. Additionally, the Irish-American actress has been also nominated for two Academy Awards, British Academy Film Awards, and the Golden Globe Awards.

Saoirse Ronan: Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Saoirse was born on 12 April 1994, in The Bronx, New York City, New York, United States. She is the only child of Monica and Paul Ronan.

From the beginning of her childhood, she had a keen interest in acting as she began to learn from a very early age. Talking about her nationality, she holds Irish and American citizenship and her ethnicity is Irish.

Moving towards her education, she attended a local school. Later, she started to study in her home with a private tutor.

Saoirse Ronan: Career, Net Worth, Awards

Saoirse Ronan began her career on Irish national broadcaster RTÉ, in the 2003 prime-time medical drama The Clinic. One year later, she appeared in another TV series named Proof. She made her debut in a movie from the 2007 film I Could Never Be Your Woman.

In the same year, she also appeared in a couple of movies, The Christmas Miracle of Jonathan Toomey, Atonement, and Death Defying Acts.

She got huge recognition after appearing in the big hit Atonement which she featured along with Keira Knightley and James McAvoy. Besides this, she also earned nominations for a BAFTA Award, a Golden Globe Award, and an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.

Later in 2010, she played the role of Irena Zielińska in the movie The Way Back. Furthermore, she is regularly kept featuring in many movies which consist of How I Live Now, The Grand Budapest Hotel, Brooklyn, Lost River, and much more. Lost River was Ryan Gosling‘s directorial debut in which she also gained huge appreciation.

In 2017, she landed various roles in a couple of movies which include Loving Vincent, Lady Bird, and On Chesil Beach. Apart from acting, she has also appeared in a few music videos which consist of Garden’s Heart by Bat for Lashes, Cherry Wine by Hozier, and Galway Girl by Ed Sheeran.

Being a renowned actress, she pockets a handsome amount of money from her profession. Currently, she has a net worth of $7 million as of 2022.

So far, Saoirse has won numerous awards which include Saturn Award for The Lovely Bones and British Independent Film Award for Brooklyn. Additionally, she has also received the Critics Choice Award, CFCA Award for Lady Bird, Sierra Award, and much more. Moreover, she is also a six-time IFTA Award winner.
