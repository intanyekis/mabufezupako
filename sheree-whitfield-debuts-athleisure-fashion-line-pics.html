Sheree Whitfield Debuts Athleisure Fashion Line: Pics

August 17, 2024
blog

It’s finally here! Shereé Whitfield is sending Us into a frenzy with her first athleisure line for her fashion label, She by Shereé.

The Real Housewives of Atlanta alum, 48, gave Us Weekly an exclusive sneak peek at her 2019 Spring Summer Collection on Monday, November 5.

“I can’t begin to express how humbled, excited, grateful and genuinely happy I am right now! It’s about time!” Whitfield, who hasn’t released new clothing under her label since 2009, exclusively told Us. “I created She by Shereé to help women find fashionable yet comfortable, high-quality everyday clothing that can be worn from day to night. I want women to feel strong, beautiful and confident when they slip into She by Shereé clothing.”

The Bravo personality, who revealed during the RHOA 10 reunion in April that she’d be dropping a line featuring joggers, added: “This has been an amazing journey and I am excited to see how She by Shereé can help complement every woman’s super power. I conceptualized and developed this collection from start to finish. I’m a perfectionist. I don’t believe in rushing things — greatness takes time and the She by Shereé 2019 Spring Summer Collection is something I am truly proud of. She is bold, she is fearless, she is you!”

Whitfield’s line caused tension during the reunion episode after Andy Cohen inquired about when it would debut. “What happened to She by Shereé?” Cohen, 50, asked at the time. Whitfield simply replied, “Joggers.”
