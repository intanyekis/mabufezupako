What is Steph Curry’s Net Worth?

Steph Curry, a professional basketball player, brand endorser, and venture capital investor, has a net worth of $160 million. He is considered the best shooter in NBA history and has revolutionized the sport with his ability to make long-range 3-point shots.

Stephen was drafted in the first round of the 2009 NBA Draft by the Golden State Warriors and has helped lead the team to NBA Championships in 2015, 2017, 2018, and 2022. He is also regarded as one of the best players of all time.

Steph Curry’s Rise to Multi-Millionaire Status

In his early years in the NBA, Steph Curry earned around $3 million per year from NBA and endorsement deals. However, today he is one of the highest-paid celebrities worldwide, earning around $80-90 million annually.

Between 2016 and 2019, Steph earned a total of $206.9 million from salary and endorsements. He has partnered with top brands such as Under Armour, Nike, and TCL’s line of “Palm” phones. Additionally, Curry is a leading investor in the Palm device and plays an active role in its testing and development.

Over the course of his first decade in the NBA, Steph earned approximately $130 million in salary and $150 million from endorsements, cementing his place as a multi-millionaire athlete and businessman.

Steph Curry Signs Record-Breaking Contract Extension with the Warriors

On August 4, 2021, it was announced that Steph Curry had signed a 4-year $215 million extension with the Golden State Warriors. This new contract extension makes him the highest-paid player in NBA history, surpassing his previous record-breaking contract.

The breakdown of the per season salary is as follows: Curry will earn $48 million in the first year of the extension, followed by $43.8 million, $47.6 million, and $51.9 million in the subsequent years. This new deal ensures that Curry will remain with the Warriors through the 2025-2026 season.

Curry has been an integral part of the Warriors’ success over the years, leading them to three NBA championships and being named the league’s Most Valuable Player twice. His impact on and off the court has made him one of the most recognizable and marketable players in the league.

With this new contract extension, Curry’s net worth is expected to continue to increase, making him one of the wealthiest players in NBA history.

Stephen Curry: Early Life and Basketball Career

Wardell Stephen Curry II was born on March 14th, 1988 in Akron, Ohio. His father, Dell Curry, was a successful NBA player who played for the Charlotte Hornets and was the all-time leading scorer for the team. While his father played for the Raptors, Curry moved to Toronto and attended Queensway Christian College where he led the basketball team to an undefeated season and won the provincial championship with the Toronto 5-0.

After Dell retired from professional basketball, the family moved back to Charlotte where Stephen Curry finished high school and led his team to three conference titles.

Stephen Curry’s College Basketball Career

Stephen Curry initially wanted to play for Virginia Tech, but the school’s basketball department was unsure about his ability to succeed due to his small stature. He instead attended Davidson College and became one of the NCAA’s highest scorers, setting new records for scoring three-pointers. He won his first NCAA Tournament with the Wildcats in his second year. In his junior year, Curry focused on developing as a point guard and finished as the leading scorer in the NCAA.

Stephen Curry’s NBA Journey

Stephen Curry joined the Golden State Warriors in 2009 and quickly became a contender for Rookie of the Year. He established himself as a free-throw specialist and won the skills challenge during All-Star Weekend. Despite struggling with issues related to his right ankle, Curry continued to perform well and broke records for completed three-pointers.

After signing a new contract with the Warriors, critics questioned the decision due to Curry’s injury-prone past. However, he proved to be a valuable member of the team and helped the Warriors win championships in the 2014-2015 and 2015-2016 seasons. Despite injuries to his left hand in 2020, Curry continued to perform at a high level.

Under new head coach Steve Kerr, the Warriors implemented a revolutionary new tactic that emphasized three-pointers over physical play underneath the hoop. This tactic spread to other teams and helped the Warriors become a title-winning force.

Personal Life of Steph Curry

Stephen Curry has been married to Ayesha Alexander since 2011 and they have three children together. He is a devout Christian and is vocal about his faith.

Outside of basketball, Curry is an avid amateur golfer and participates in celebrity golf tournaments, including playing alongside Barack Obama. He is also a fan of English Premier League soccer club Chelsea F.C.

Steph’s younger brother, Seth Curry, is also a professional basketball player.

Steph Curry’s Real Estate Investments

In 2015, Steph Curry bought a home in Walnut, California for $3.2 million.

He then owned a home in Alamo, California which he bought in 2016 for $5.775 million and sold in January 2019 for $6.3 million.

In September 2019, Steph and Ayesha Curry purchased a brand new home in Atherton, California for $31 million. The mansion sits on 1.2 acres and features a detached garage and separate guest house. The couple sold the house in September 2021 for $31.15 million.

In December 2020, the Currys bought a newly constructed, 18,000 square foot mansion in Atherton for $30 million on two acres.

Most recently, in July 2022, the Currys paid $2.1 million for a home located in Winter Park, Florida.
