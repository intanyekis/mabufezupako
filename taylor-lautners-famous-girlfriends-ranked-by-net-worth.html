5 How Much Is Marie Avgeropoulos Worth?

During the mid-2010s, Taylor Lautner dated Marie Avgeropoulos for over a year in 2013 and 2014. Considering that Avgeropoulos later was charged with felony domestic violence, that is a really good thing. 4 How Much Is Billie Lourd Worth?

Before Taylor Lautner got engaged, his last confirmed girlfriend was Billie Lourd who he dated for six months in 2016 and 2017. Lucky enough to have developed a relationship with Ryan Murphy, Lourd starred in his show Scream Queens and she has appeared in several seasons of his series American Horror Story. On top of that, Lourd also notably portrayed Lieutenant Connix in the Star Wars sequel trilogy. Of course, that fact makes sense since even though Lourd is an accomplished actor in her own right, she also is the daughter of Carrie Fisher. Thanks to all of the work she has done as an actor and anything she inherited after her mom passed, Lourd has a $20 million fortune according to celebritynetworth.com.

3 How Much Is Lily Collins Worth?

Evidently, Taylor Lautner is attracted to the daughters of legends. After all, on top of dating Carrie Fisher’s daughter, for almost a year in 2010 and 2011 he also dated the daughter of the legendary singer and drummer Phil Collins, Lily Collins. Of course, just like Billie Lourd, Lily is a lot more than just the daughter of Phil Collins. An extremely successful actor, Lily has starred in a long list of movies including Mirror Mirror, The Mortal Instruments: City of Bones, Okja, as well as Extremely Wicked, Shockingly Evil and Vile. Mostly due to those roles, Lily has a net worth of $25 million according to celebritynetworth.com.

Of all the people to appear in this list, Taylor Lautner dated Selena Gomez the earliest as they were linked from April until July of 2009. After starting her career as a child, Gomez went on to star in Wizards of Waverly Place for five years between 2007 and 2012. After that show ended, Gomez moved on to movie roles In films like the Hotel Transylvania series, Spring Breakers, and Monte Carlo among others. Also a very successful pop star, Gomez has released a long list of hit songs including “Come & Get It”, “Lose You to Love Me”, “The Heart Wants What It Wants”, and “Love You Like a Love Song”. Given all of the success she has enjoyed, it shouldn't surprise anyone that according to celebritynetworth.com, Gomez has an impressive $85 million fortune.
